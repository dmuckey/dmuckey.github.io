David's Recipes (work in progress)

Makes 2 servings (ingredients):
  • Butter (4 oz)
  • Fresh (high quality) pasta (8 oz)
  • 1 oz truffle of your choice (you may have some leftover)
  • Truffle oil (enough to drizzle)
  • Parsley (just a pinch)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
    1. In preparation, begin by shaving the truffle into thin slices and bringing a pot of salted water to a boil
    2. Add the pasta and cook for the appropriate amount of time according to the packaging
    3. While the pasta is cooking, melt the butter on low heat along with 3/4 of the shaved truffle. Allow the butter and truffle to simmer together on low to low/medium heat for about 10-15 minutes
      4. This is the time where a very nice aroma will begin to fill your residence and potentially make neighbors jealous
    4. Once pasta has finished cooking, drain the pasta keeping ½ cup of pasta water to help thicken the sauce
    5. Now mix the cooked pasta, butter with truffle, and the ½ cup of pasta water together in a sauté pan until truffle sauce is well mixed and has thickened. Add salt to taste
    6. The dish is now ready to be served! Drizzle a little truffle oil on top, add a healthy portion of shaved truffle (from the remaining 1/4) and add a pinch of parsley and parmesan cheese
